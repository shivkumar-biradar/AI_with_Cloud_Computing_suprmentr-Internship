Designed Solution: Smart Attendance System

Problem:
Manual attendance takes time.

Solution:
Use face detection to automatically mark attendance.

Steps:
1. Capture image
2. Detect face
3. Match with stored data
4. Mark attendance

Benefits:
- Saves time
- Accurate
- No manual work
